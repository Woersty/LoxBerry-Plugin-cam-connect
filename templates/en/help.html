With LoxBerry watermark: Prints a LoxBerry watermark in the left lower corner.

Send eMail with picture: Sends an eMail with the camera picture if the Plugin is called.

  • Recipient list: eMail-Address of the der recipients. Multiple separated by semicolon.
  • Sender name: Name but not eMail-Address of the sender. The settings for the sender are made in the LoxBerry Mainmenu under Mailserver.
  • Subject: The subject line of the eMail. Consists of multiple parts. The 2nd fiels is treated as date and the 4th as time. Details see PHP-Onlinehelp
  • Text: eMail text. HTML is allowed. Details see HTML-Onlinehelp. Line breaks with <br/>
  • Picture not as attachment but inline: Sends the Camera picture not as an attachment but put it under the text before the signature.
  • Get eMail recipients from request URL: When calling the plugin from Loxone Config, the recipients-eMailaddress it submitted with &email_to=me@email.com.
  • Imagewidth in the eMail: Size of the picture in the eMail. Height is automatically calculated to keep aspect ratio.
  • Signature: eMail-Signature. HTML is allowed here too.
